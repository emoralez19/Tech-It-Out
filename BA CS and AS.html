B.A. in Computer Science and Application Systems


The B.A. in Computer Science and Application Systems at St. Mary's University provides a comprehensive educational journey where students explore the intricacies of computer science in tandem with practical application systems. This program instills a solid foundation in programming, databases, and software engineering, fostering a profound understanding of both theoretical concepts and hands-on implementation. Graduates are well-equipped to navigate the dynamic intersection of computer science and application systems, poised for success in roles ranging from software development to adept system analysis.
