Certificate in Educational Computer Gaming


In this graduate certificate program, students will learn to design, create, and evaluate educational computer games. Students will create at least two educational games and learn about the gamification of instruction throughout the courses of this certificate.

To participate in a graduate certificate, students must have an undergraduate degree in a techology- related field (or equivalent experience) and have completed at least two computer programming courses with a B- or better.



Required Courses
Hours
CS 6335 Game Development 3
CS 6345 eLearning and Gamification 3
 
 
 
 
Elective Classes
Select two of the following: Hours
CS 6315 Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning* 3
CS 6320 Files and Database* 3
CS 6325 Computer Graphics* 3
CS 6330 Advanced Computer Networks* 3
CS 6350 Hardware & Operating Systems* 3
CS 6375 Special Topics* 3 
*Any research work in these classes must be related to educational gaming*



Total Hours: 12
