B.S. in Computer Science




The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science is the more mathematically rigorous of the two BS degrees. This program encompasses a wide range of topics fundamental to computer systems. With a strong focus on programming, students develop essential coding skills. Alongside programming, the degree delves into algorithms, software engineering, databases, and more, expanding students' understanding of computer systems and their practical application in various industries.
