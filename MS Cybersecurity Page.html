M.S. in Cybersecurity


The Cybersecurity Master's Program at St. Mary's University is one of the many programs offered by the Computer Science Department. Headed by Dr. Ayad Barsoum this program goes over the many cybersecurity threats via hands on experience. This master's program shares similarities with our BS Computer Science Cybersecurity track, the program delves deeper into the subject matter. Through comprehensive coursework and hands-on experiences, students gain a thorough understanding of digital systems and data threats. This immersive approach not only familiarizes students with the material but also allows them to witness the real-world impact of various cyber threats firsthand.
