M.S. in Computer Information Systems


The Master of Science in Computer Information Systems program at St. Mary's University, under the direction of Dr. Art Hanna, equips students with comprehensive knowledge to oversee the life cycle of software development projects. Covering key aspects including hardware organization, data communication, databases, software requirements analysis, design methodologies, and software implementation and testing, the program ensures a well-rounded understanding of computer systems. The faculty brings expertise in artificial intelligence, computer security/cybersecurity, game development, and programming languages, providing students with a solid foundation for navigating these specialized domains.
