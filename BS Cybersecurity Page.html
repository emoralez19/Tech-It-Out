B.S. in Computer Science Cybersecurity Track


The Cybersecurity Track within the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program is a specialized track offered at St. Mary's University, led by Dr. Ayad Barsoum. This track is designed to equip students with the necessary skills to safeguard digital systems and data against cyber threats. Through hands-on learning experiences, students explore a wide range of security domains, including encryption, network security, and ethical hacking. The curriculum features practical lab sessions where students gain firsthand experience in executing ethical hacking techniques.
